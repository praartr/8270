1. Small letters [a-z] and digits [0-9] are encoded into leet symbols. 
2. Capital Letters are not encoded into a leet symbols. They display as such since having the same symbol for small and caps of the same letter will lead to ambiguity while decoding.

Pizzazz:

1. The digits are encoded into words such as "9" would encode to "nine".
2. The percent (%) symbol will be encoded in words as "percent"
